# Working with Prompts

## Fundamentals

- **Be Specific and Detailed** - clear, explicit instructions produce better results than vague requests
- **Provide Context** - share relevant background, constraints, and goals upfront
- **Use Examples** - show what you want with concrete examples when possible
- **Break Down Complex Tasks** - divide large requests into smaller, manageable steps
- **Iterate and Refine** - start with a basic prompt, then refine based on results
